Synopsis

The Ghost is a action thriller movie directed by Praveen Sattaru. The movie casts Nagarjuna in the lead role. The Music composed by Bharatt - Saurabh while cinematography done by Mukesh G and it is edited by Dharmendra Kakarala. T he film is produced by Narayan Das K Narang, Pushkar Ram Mohan Rao and Sharrath Marar.

Cast

Nagarjuna Akkineni,Kajal Aggarwal and Sonal Chaushan play the lead roles in this movie.

Crew

Directed by Praveen Sattaru and cinematography by Mukesh G.
