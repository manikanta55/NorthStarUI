Synopsis

Soon after newlyweds Ajay and Geetha are brutally murdered, an unknown injured man arrives at their families' doorstep. Can he be the killer, or is there a twist to the tale? Find out in Gaalivaana.

Cast

Radhika Sarathkumar and Sai Kumar play the lead roles in this series. This also features Chandini Chowdary, Nandini Rai, Chaitanya Krishna, Thagubothu Ramesh, Jyothi Pradeep, and Ashritha Vemuganti.

Crew

Directed by Sharan Koppisetty and cinematography by Sujatha Siddhartha.
